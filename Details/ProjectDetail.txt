2. Project Overview

Collabzy is an online platform designed to connect social media influencers with brands or individuals who want to promote their products or services.

The platform acts as a marketplace where:

Influencers showcase their promotional services.

Brands search, contact, and collaborate with suitable influencers.

The objective is to simplify influencer marketing by providing a structured and transparent system instead of informal social media messaging.

3. Problem Statement

In the current market:

Brands struggle to find the right influencers for their products.

Influencers find it difficult to get genuine paid collaborations.

Communication happens through scattered messages with no proper tracking.

There is no clear system for pricing, progress, or accountability.

These issues lead to wasted time, misunderstandings, and low trust on both sides.

4. Proposed Solution

Collabzy provides a single platform where:

Influencers and brands can connect professionally.

Collaboration details are clearly defined.

The entire process is organized, transparent, and trackable.

This reduces confusion and improves trust between both parties.

5. Objectives

To create a digital meeting place for influencers and brands

To reduce manual effort in finding collaborations

To provide clarity in pricing and service offerings

To improve transparency and reliability in influencer marketing

6. Target Users

Social media creators from different niches

b) Brands / Product Owners
